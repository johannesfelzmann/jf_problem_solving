About me

Johannes
Felzmann

I'm a software developer, lecturer and consultant with a master's degree in Logic & Computation from TU Wien. I help businesses solve problems, build smart software and understand the technology behind it.

At JF Problem Solving, my mission is simple: make complex technology accessible. Whether it's a dense theoretical concept, a cutting-edge AI framework, or a tangled software architecture, I believe every technical challenge can be broken down into clear, natural solutions that anyone can understand and act on. With a background spanning formal logic, full-stack development, and hands-on teaching, I bring both the depth to tackle hard problems and the communication skills to explain them without jargon. No matter the industry or scale, from startups finding their footing to established teams modernizing their stack, I work to understand your specific business context and deliver solutions that are practical, elegant, and built to last.

I believe every problem has an elegant solution. Finding it is the part I enjoy most.

Outside of computer science, I compete in triathlon at a professional level where I focus on log distance racing.
